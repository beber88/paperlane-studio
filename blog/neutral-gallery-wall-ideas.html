The formula for a calm gallery wall

  • Stick to one palette: warm whites, cream, soft black lines.
  • Odd numbers feel natural: 3 or 5 pieces on a narrow wall, 6–9 above a sofa or bed.
  • Mix subjects, not styles: a face, a botanical, an abstract — all in the same line-art style.

3 layouts that always work

The grid: 6 prints, 2 rows of 3, identical frames. Clean and hotel-like.
The organic cluster: different frame sizes, 5 cm gaps, center at eye level.
The ledge: lean 3–4 framed prints on a picture ledge — zero nail holes.

Why line art works in every room

Single-line drawings add character without visual noise — ideal for bedrooms, nurseries and home offices where you want warmth and calm.
