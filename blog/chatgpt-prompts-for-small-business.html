12 ChatGPT Prompts Every Small Business Owner Should Steal

Most people get bland results from AI because they write bland prompts. These 12 free prompts are engineered to give usable output in one shot — copy, paste, replace the [BRACKETS].

Marketing

  • "Generate 20 short-form hooks for [TOPIC] aimed at [AUDIENCE]. Mix curiosity, contrarian and result-driven angles."
  • "Turn this one idea into a week of content: 1 long post, 3 short posts, 1 email: [IDEA]."
  • "Write 10 Pinterest pin titles for [PRODUCT], keyword-rich, under 60 characters."

Sales

  • "Write a product description for [PRODUCT] that leads with the transformation, not features."
  • "List 7 reasons a skeptical buyer would hesitate to buy [PRODUCT], and a one-line reframe for each."
  • "Write 3 versions of a call-to-action for [OFFER]: urgent, friendly, premium."

Strategy

  • "Act as a no-nonsense advisor. Here's my situation: [DESCRIBE]. Ask me 5 sharp questions before advising."
  • "Build a 30-day launch plan for [PRODUCT], one action per day, 1 hour per day."
  • "What are the 3 most likely reasons this plan fails, and the cheapest safeguard for each? [PLAN]"

These 12 are from our full collection of 150 prompts covering offers, content, sales pages, SEO, customer service and weekly planning.
